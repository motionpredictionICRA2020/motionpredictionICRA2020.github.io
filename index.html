2nd Workshop on Long-term Human Motion Prediction

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), 2 June 2020, 16:30 - 20:30 CEST Online

With this workshop we bring together an interdisciplinary community of high-profile researchers and industry leaders, interested in long-term human motion prediction

Dynamic Environments

Predicting future states, actions and behavior of dynamic agents

Growing Community

Researchers' interest to motion prediction has grown considerably in the recent years

Advanced Methods

Human motion prediction encapsulates a wide variety of methods

Safety

Motion prediction is imperative for safety of automonous vehicles

Shared Spaces

Understanding and forecasting human activity is required for robot operation in shared spaces

Human-Robot Interaction

Motion prediction is essential for close-proximity human-robot collaboration


Workshop topics

  • Motion trajectory prediction in 2D and 3D
  • Predicting articulated human motion
  • Early action and activity recognition
  • Motion and Task Planning in dynamic environments considering motion predictions
  • Anticipation of group and crowd motion
  • Human motion prediction and safety
  • Human-Robot Interaction considering predictions
  • Evaluation of prediction algorithms: datasets, metrics and benchmarks
  • Predictive planning and control
  • Applications of motion prediction techniques
  • Visual scene prediction

Workshop highlights

  • 7 engaging talks
  • Application-driven discussion
  • Call for papers / Poster session
  • Motion prediction challenge
