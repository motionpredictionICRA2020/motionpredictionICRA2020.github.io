Call For Papers

We welcome researchers in the field to submit papers to be presented as posters. Submitted manuscripts should be up to 2 pages plus references, formatted according to ICRA standards using the Paper Template downloadable on the IEEE ICRA 2020 website (two-column format). We encourage authors to submit a video clip to complement their manuscript. Submissions will be selected based on their originality, relevance to the workshop topics, contributions, technical clarity, and presentation.

To submit your contributions please follow: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=lhmp2020

Topics

  • Motion trajectory prediction in 2D and 3D
  • Predicting articulated human motion
  • Early action and activity recognition
  • Motion and Task Planning in dynamic environments considering motion predictions
  • Anticipation of group and crowd motion
  • Human motion prediction and safety
  • Human-Robot Interaction considering predictions
  • Evaluation of prediction algorithms: datasets, metrics and benchmarks
  • Predictive planning and control
  • Applications of motion prediction techniques
  • Visual scene prediction

New deadlines!!
Submission: May 20, 2020, 23:59 CEST
Notification: May 27, 2020

Presenter instructions

Each accepted paper will be presented as a virtual poster in a dedicated room during the 30 minute session. The posters will be made public on this website shortly before the workshop, and announced at the main workshop track. The virtual poster should contain 3 slides in a pdf format, which could be used for presetnting and answering questions from the audience in the same way as a physical poster. Apart from that, during the workshop session presenters are free to use any supporting materials.
